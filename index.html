The Class

Move. Breathe. Connect.

Simple practices for a slower, more intentional life. We gather across Seoul to move, breathe, sit in stillness, and share a cup of tea together. Open to all backgrounds, languages, and levels of experience.

  • 01

    English & Korean

    Guided in both languages — clear, friendly cues whether it's your first class or your hundredth.

  • 02

    Open to every body

    Beginners are the heart of this class. Come as you are — modifications offered throughout.

  • 03

    Tea after every class

    We close each session with warm tea and easy conversation — the real reason it's "Yoga & Tea."
