The Class

Breathe with the river, not the rush.

Seoul Yoga & Tea is a weekly practice for anyone who wants to start the weekend slow. We move through a gentle, all-levels flow — guided entirely in English — and then share a pot of tea together. By the Han River in spring and autumn, indoors in Mapo through summer and winter. The same calm, every season.

  • 01

    Everything in English

    No Korean needed. Clear, friendly cues whether it's your first class or your hundredth.

  • 02

    Open to every body

    Beginners are the heart of this class. Come as you are — modifications offered throughout.

  • 03

    Tea after every class

    We close each session with warm tea and easy conversation — the real reason it's "Yoga & Tea."
